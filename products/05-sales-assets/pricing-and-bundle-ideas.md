# Pricing and Bundle Ideas

## Standalone Pricing

- `JEE Rank Accelerator Blueprint`: Rs. 499 to Rs. 999
- `Study Systems Pack`: Rs. 199 to Rs. 399
- `Mistake Book Templates`: Rs. 199 to Rs. 399
- `AI Student Toolkit`: Rs. 149 to Rs. 299

## Bundle Pricing

- `JEE Execution OS Lite`
  Blueprint + Study Systems Pack
  Suggested price: Rs. 699 to Rs. 999

- `JEE Execution OS Complete`
  All 4 products
  Suggested price: Rs. 999 to Rs. 1499

## Positioning Logic

- The blueprint is the anchor product
- The systems pack reduces implementation friction
- The mistake book adds differentiation
- The AI toolkit feels modern and high-value

## Launch Strategy

Lead with the pain:

`Most students do not need more information. They need a system that turns effort into marks.`

Then show the transformation:

`From backlog, random studying, and bad test analysis to a visible weekly improvement system.`
