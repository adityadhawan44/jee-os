## Concept Clarity

1. Explain `[chapter/concept]` at JEE Main level with intuition first, then formulas, then 5 common traps.
2. Explain `[chapter/concept]` at JEE Advanced level and show how questions usually become tricky.
3. Teach me `[chapter/concept]` like I am weak at basics but serious about improving in 7 days.
4. Compare `[concept A]` and `[concept B]` for JEE and show where students confuse them.
5. Give me a zero-to-strong roadmap for `[chapter]` in 5 study sessions.
6. Turn `[chapter]` into a one-page revision sheet with formulas, exceptions, and common mistakes.
7. Teach `[concept]` using examples that move from easy to hard.
8. Make me answer questions while you teach `[concept]` instead of giving a lecture.
9. Explain why `[concept]` matters inside actual JEE questions.
10. Show me the minimum theory I must know before solving problems from `[chapter]`.

14. Give me mixed questions from `[chapter 1]`, `[chapter 2]`, and `[chapter 3]` to test whether I can distinguish concepts.
15. Make 10 assertion-reason or statement-based questions on `[concept]`.
16. Give me 20 objective questions on `[chapter]` designed to expose common mistakes.
17. Create 5 multi-concept questions combining `[chapter A]` and `[chapter B]`.
